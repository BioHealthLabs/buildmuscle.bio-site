Muscle Building FAQ

When should I take protein powder?

Most people take protein immediately after training, or in between meals to support muscle recovery.

What’s the best creatine to take?

Creatine monohydrate is the most studied and proven form, but creatine HMB offers additional benefits for preserving muscle mass.

How much protein do I need?

Aim for 1.6–2.2 g protein per kg body weight per day to maximize muscle gain.
